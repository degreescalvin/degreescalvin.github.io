Japan itinerary

After hearing about the plethora of stamps available in Japan, I was inspired to use my skills to create a personalised itinerary for a recent trip to Tokyo and Osaka. This itinerary was to be the home for the beautiful stamps and souvenirs I collected whilst overseas.

Editorial


WIP The Opua – The History of a Ship for Many Years a Landmark on the Wairarapa Coast

In 1926 the Opua wrecked at Tora Coast just outside of Wellington. This Opua was a record of the events of what was, for many years, a landmark on the Wairarapa Coast. First published by Peter Deller over 60 years ago, it is now the project of Katie Deller and I to restore and republish it once again.

Home

While the creation of the Emissions Trading Scheme (ETS) has good intentions, one unintended consequence of this scheme was the perception of easy money by lifestyle farmers. Home was created as a way to visualise the complex rules, roles and obligations for would-be participants.
